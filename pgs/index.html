Additional Learning Content

This additional learning content aims to enable you to:

Refresh and extend your knowledge, skills and critical understanding of advanced research methods. Creatively apply the principles of quantitative research methods to research and practice. Refresh and extend your skills in project design, management, analysis and presentation. This content is also designed to help prepare you for any specific research projects (Dissertation) involved in undergraduate study(final year), masters level and a important aspect of doctoral level PhD programmes. This additional content should aid to ensure that you have the required knowledge and skills to successfully engage with those modules/projects.

    The key foci for this content include:

  • Basic research concepts revision.
  • Research design and development.
  • Data processing and analysis.
    • The additional content is taught using a variety of techniques we hope (if enganged with) will provide a gradual means to improve your knowledge, understanding and approach to research theory and methods. This content may include include lectures, guided statistical analysis and tutorials with added task books and examples for practicing.
